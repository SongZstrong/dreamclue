## Introduction

DreamClue AI uses cookies and similar technologies to keep the site working, remember preferences, protect accounts, and understand product usage.

## Essential Cookies

Essential cookies support login sessions, security checks, language preference, and basic site functionality. The service may not work correctly without them.

## Analytics and Performance

If enabled, analytics cookies help us understand which pages and features are used so we can improve dream journaling and interpretation workflows.

## Managing Cookies

Most browsers let you block or delete cookies. Blocking essential cookies may prevent account login, dream journal access, or saved preferences from working.
