## Introduction

DreamClue AI is built for personal dream journaling and AI-assisted interpretation. Dream entries can contain sensitive memories, emotions, and personal symbols, so privacy is central to the service.

## Information We Collect

We may collect account information such as name and email address, dream journal content you choose to save, interpretation requests, usage data, device information, payment status, and communication you send through contact forms.

## How We Use Information

We use this information to provide dream journaling, Dreambook search, AI interpretation, account access, payment processing, support, abuse prevention, and product improvement.

## Dream Content

Dream entries remain personal journal content associated with your account. We do not treat AI interpretation as medical or clinical advice.

## Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for authentication, email, payments, storage, analytics, and AI processing. These providers are used only to operate the service.

## Contact

For privacy questions, please [contact us](/contact).
