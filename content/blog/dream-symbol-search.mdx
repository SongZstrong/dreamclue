Dream symbol search should not stop at matching one word. If you search for "water", a useful system should distinguish calm lakes, rising floods, deep oceans, rain, and blocked crossings.

DreamClue AI uses the dream text as a whole. It looks for related symbols, emotional tone, and narrative structure, then returns passages and interpretation notes that are closer to the dream you actually described.

This matters because the same symbol can point in opposite directions. A house may represent safety in one dream and confinement in another. A staircase may feel hopeful when you climb it and frightening when it collapses.

The best search query is a short dream scene, not a single keyword. Try writing what happened, how it felt, and the object or place that stayed in memory.
